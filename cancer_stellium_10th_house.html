Primary Professional Challenges:

Becoming a parental or smothering figure professionally: Risk of overstepping boundaries with subordinates or using emotional manipulation to maintain control. They must lead with nurturing authority, not authoritarian nurturance.

How does this placement affect career choice and professional ambitions?

This relationship is central, complex, and directly impacts their public life. Traditionally, the 10th House is linked to the nurturing parent (often the mother).
