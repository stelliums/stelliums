Understanding Stelliums

A stellium occurs when three or more planets cluster in one zodiac sign or house, creating an intense concentration of energy that becomes a dominant theme in your birth chart. When this happens in Aries, the sign of initiative and courage, it creates a powerful drive to pioneer, lead, and assert your individuality in the area of life represented by the house placement.
