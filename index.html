Understanding Stelliums in Astrology

A stellium occurs when three or more planets cluster in one zodiac sign or house in your birth chart, creating an intense concentration of energy that becomes a dominant theme in your personality and life experience. This powerful configuration amplifies the qualities of the sign and house, creating a focal point of your personal expression and life path.

Stelliums represent areas of life where you have exceptional focus, talent, and challenges. They show where you pour your energy and where you're destined to learn important life lessons. Understanding your stelliums can provide profound insights into your core motivations, strengths, and areas for growth.
