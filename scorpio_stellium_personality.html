FAQ: Understanding the Scorpio Stellium Personality

They are not inherently dark, but they are comfortable with darkness. They understand that light is meaningless without shadow. How do they handle conflict?

They are strategic, not reactive, in conflict. They rarely engage in heated, spontaneous arguments. Can they ever be truly happy and lighthearted?

Their version of happiness is not carefree lightness; it is profound contentment, earned trust, and a sense of authentic personal power. The combination of secrecy, distrust, and emotional intensity can wall them off from the genuine connection they crave, creating a self-fulfilling prophecy of betrayal.
